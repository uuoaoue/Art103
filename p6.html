Phase 1 -- Sign Series -- Location Scouting and Brainstorming Signs

Signs ideas

Bay Badminton Center- Milpitas
BBC is located in a really isolated block, surrounded by large trucks, and has no signs that this is a badminton place. Therefore, the sign idea I have for Bay Badminton Center (BBC) is a sign that tells you that you are at BBC.

99 Ranch Market - Cuptertino
This is the market I usually go every week to buy some groceries. Whenever I come here, I have a hard time finding a parking spot because some of the spots is taken by the shopping carts. People do not put the carts back to the right place. So my sign idea is "Fighting with the carts" to express what is happening here.

SJSU Art Foundry - San Jose
I am taking a class at the Art Foundry this semester and what I like most about this place is its free parking. It has a big space for students who study there to park their car without paying any fees. So my sign idea is this is the "best student parking on campus" to highlight it.

Boba Bliss - Mountain View
This is their second store in the Bay. Their first one is in Dublin which is really far from me. So when they open their store in Mountain View, I go here more often. They have their signature drink called "Creme Brulee Pearl Latte", which is the best so far. Therefore, my sign idea is to advertise the drink with a sign saying "MUST TRY Creme Brulee Pearl Latte"

Phase 3 -- CAM Produce - Toolpath Simulations

Create CAM toolpaths for each of the 2 stamp models made for Phase 2.

The main details and changes for a 2d Pocket operations are:
Tool: 1/8" ( .125") flat end mill tool, speeds and feeds: set to Brass Roughing
Geometry: select pockets in your model
Heights: leave default
Passes: Change Maximum Stepover to ".0625", Select Multiple Depth, Set Maximum Roughing Stepdown to .04" , Set Finishing Stepdown to .02"
Linking: leave default
